Our Story

Founded in 2020, DipenTech started with a simple observation: most businesses were drowning in repetitive tasks that could easily be automated. Our founders, a team of AI researchers and business experts, set out to build tools that would free teams to focus on what matters most.

Today, we serve over 500 companies worldwide, from startups to Fortune 500 enterprises. Our AI-powered platform has automated millions of tasks, generated billions in pipeline value, and helped our customers achieve an average of 340% ROI.

We believe that AI should be a tool for empowerment, not replacement. Our solutions are designed to augment human capabilities, making teams more productive, decisions more informed, and businesses more competitive.
